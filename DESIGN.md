# Jarvis Dashboard Design Notes

## Palette
- Background: `#070a12`, `#0b1020`, `#0f1528` with radial neon orbs.
- Glass panes: `rgba(18, 24, 38, 0.7)` and `rgba(16, 22, 36, 0.9)`.
- Neon accents: `#61f4ff` (blue), `#43ffb6` (green), `#8d6bff` (violet).
- Text: `#eef3ff` primary, `#98a4c8` muted.

## Typography
- Display: Space Grotesk (headings, nav, labels).
- Body: Noto Sans (paragraphs, metadata).
- Uppercase micro-labels with wide letter spacing for sci-fi telemetry feel.

## Spacing
- Page gutter: `clamp(20px, 3vw, 40px)`.
- Panel padding: `clamp(18px, 3vw, 26px)` with `18px`-`28px` radii.
- Grid gaps: `12px` to `32px` based on section density.
